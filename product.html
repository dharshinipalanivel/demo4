High-Performance Industrial Valves

Our Oil & Gas Product

Our products meet API 6A, 7A, 17A, and 20E standards, ensuring safety, strength, and long service life in demanding environments.

Gate Valve

Gate valves are primarily used to start or stop the flow of fluid in pipelines. They provide a straight-line flow with minimal pressure drop and are suitable for both liquid and gas applications. Designed for full open or close operation, they ensure reliable shutoff in high-pressure systems.

Know More

Check Valve

Check valves are automatic one-way valves that allow fluid to flow in a single direction, preventing backflow and protecting pumps and compressors. They are essential for maintaining safe and efficient pipeline operations in oil, gas, and water systems.

Know More

Ball Valve

Ball valves are quarter-turn valves that use a spherical ball to control flow. Known for their durability and tight sealing, they provide smooth operation and minimal leakage. They are ideal for applications requiring quick shutoff and long service life.

Know More

Butterfly Valve

Butterfly valves are compact, lightweight, and designed for isolating or regulating flow. They feature a rotating disc for quick quarter-turn operation, making them suitable for large-diameter pipelines and applications requiring space efficiency and easy maintenance.

Know More

Needle Valve

Needle valves are precision-engineered valves used to accurately regulate small flow rates of liquids or gases. They feature a slender, tapered needle-shaped plunger that allows fine control of flow, making them ideal for instrumentation, calibration, and sampling lines in oil, gas, and chemical industries.
